About My Design

In our everyday lives we are surrounded by design; from the houses in which we live, to the cars we drive, all starting from a concept which originates on an art board of a skilled designer. One day I hope my name finds itself among the names of other successful designers and that the concepts that begin their life on my art board are viewed and appreciated by the public all over the world.

Design is all around us, every product we use in our daily lives has been designed to some degree, either well or poorly. This is one thing that I consider often and try to take into consideration when approaching any piece of work - thorough and efficient design makes products great.
