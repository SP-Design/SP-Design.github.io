Going Through Changes

This poster was designed for a school exhibition. Take a modern day phenomenon and create a poster to represent it. I chose the effect that social media has on today's youth. This scene takes inspriation from the cult classic, film adaptiation of Stephan Kings 'IT' but with social media luring in unsuspecting children, instead of sinister clowns, and the letting go of the balloon representing the loss of child like innocence.

Developed by Stefan Pollin, 2019
